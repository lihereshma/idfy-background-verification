Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why should I conduct background checks on my employees?

    Background verification validates the authenticity of the information provided by your candidate, reducing the risk of potential employee fraud. More importantly, it enables you to hire trustworthy people. It answers questions like, "Is this candidate who he says he is? Are the documents provided legit? Have they committed a crime before? And a lot more…

    When individuals are aware of background checks, it can deter those with dishonest intentions and promote a safe work environment.

  • What are the typical background checks that companies usually perform?

    Companies typically conduct checks on aspects like employment history, reference checks, education qualifications, criminal history, credit history, and address verification.

    These are standard across most firms, from small companies to large enterprises. They provide a comprehensive overview of the candidate’s professional history and help make better hiring decisions.

  • What is digital background verification?

    Digital background verification is the process of conducting background checks using digital platforms and technologies.

    With digital verification, the physical address check that used to take a week can now be completed within a day via a video call. These digital alternatives help for a quicker and more efficient verification process compared to traditional methods.

  • Does IDfy offer digital background verification?

    Yes, we do. To ensure the fastest TAT in the industry, we initially conduct all checks digitally.

    Here are some of the digital equivalents of the standard checks:

    • Address verification: Agent-assisted, digital address verification (DAVe)
    • Employment check: EPFO (details fetched on PF online portal)
    • Qualification check: DigiLocker (Education documents fetched from Digilocker with the consent of the candidate)
    • Criminal check: Digital court record checks + eFIR database

    However, we can also conduct all these checks in the traditional manner by reaching out to HRs and universities.
